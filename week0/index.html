DAMASCUS: City Of History


General Information:

Damascus is the capital of Syria,it is located in the south-western part of Syria.
It is also known as ash-sham and Dimashq.
It is one of the oldest cities in the world and was first settled in around 2000 BC,an evidence exists of settlement in the wider Barada basin dating back to 9000 BC.
Many nations lived in Damascus including Aramaeans and Arabs.

Here is a list shows some of the stages in wich Damascus passed through and the period of each stage:


The Era Start End
Greco-Roman period 64 BC 636 AD
Islamic Arab Era 636 AD 1079 AD
Seljuq-Ayyubid rule 1079 AD 1260 AD
Mamluk period 1260 AD 1516 AD
Ottoman rule 1516 AD 1918 AD
Modern period 1918 AD now
